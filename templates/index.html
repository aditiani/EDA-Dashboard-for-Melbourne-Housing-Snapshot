Is there any correlation between price and distance?

  • The hypothesis are H0 means there is no correlation and H1 means there is a correlation.
  • I use Spearman's Correlation Coefficient test to summarize the linear relationship between two variables because the data isn't in Normal Distribution.
  • In this test, the p-value is not greater than 0.05, so the H0 hypothesis is rejected, which means there is a correlation but it's a small correlation. I can see that the two variables are negatively correlated and the correlation is -0.13.
